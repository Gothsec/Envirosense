About us

Envirosense is an innovative virtual platform designed to address the problem of inefficient energy consumption in homes and businesses. Our application allows users to track their energy consumption in detail and receive personalized recommendations to improve energy efficiency. With interactive features and intuitive graphics, Envirosense makes it easy to understand consumption patterns and promote more sustainable practices. In addition, we are committed to ensuring data privacy and developing ethically, prioritizing collective benefit over individual interests. With Envirosense, we are driving a shift towards a more sustainable and energy-conscious future!
